# About LACK

**LACK (Lobbying Against Climate Knowledge)** is a research project developing a knowledge graph of the individuals and organisations involved in lobbying against climate science awareness and climate policy. It is part of the [ClimateSense](https://github.com/climatesense-project) project at the Knowledge Media Institute (KMi).

The knowledge graph is built from a large corpus of real-world relation expressions extracted from investigative journalism sources, and is described using a purpose-built lightweight ontology.

---

## Sources

LACK is built primarily from two investigative sources:

| Source | Coverage |
|---|---|
| [Desmog](https://www.desmog.com/) | 943 person and organisation profile pages |
| [LobbyMap / InfluenceMap](https://lobbymap.org/) | 964 entries (league table) + 338 linked by |

Additional candidate sources are under consideration. 

---

## Status

LACK is an active research project. Version 1.0 of the knowledge graph was released on 23 April 2026, comprising. The project is currently a resource paper submission to [ISWC 2026](https://iswc2026.semanticweb.org/).
